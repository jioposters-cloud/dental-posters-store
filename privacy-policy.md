# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 2025

## Introduction

Status Ring ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and make purchases.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Shipping address
- Billing address
- Payment information (processed securely)

### Non-Personal Information
- Browser type
- IP address
- Pages visited
- Time and date of visit
- Cookies and tracking data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To process and fulfill your orders
- To send order confirmations and updates
- To respond to customer inquiries
- To improve our website and services
- To send promotional emails (with your consent)
- To prevent fraud and ensure security

## 3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All transactions are encrypted and processed securely through our payment provider (Snipcart).

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use **Snipcart** for payment processing. They maintain their own privacy policy and security standards. We do not share your payment details with anyone else.

## 5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

- Request deletion of your account
- Opt-out of promotional communications

Contact us at **thestatusring@gmail.com** to exercise these rights.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
