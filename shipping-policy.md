# Shipping Policy

## Delivery Timeline

- **Delhi/NCR:** 2-3 business days
- **Metro Cities:** 3-4 business days  
- **Other Cities:** 5-7 business days
- **Remote Areas:** 7-14 business days

## Shipping Charges

- **Free Shipping:** Orders above ₹500 (within India)
- **Flat Rate:** ₹50 for orders below ₹500

## Order Processing

- Orders are processed within 24 hours (working days)
- You'll receive a tracking number via WhatsApp/Email
- Your order will be packed with care

## Packaging

All products are carefully packaged to prevent damage during transit:
- Rolled posters in protective tubes
- 3D prints in foam-padded boxes
- Books in secure mailers

## Tracking Your Order

- All orders include tracking
- Track your shipment using the provided number
- You can also contact us on WhatsApp for updates

## Delivery Issues

If your order doesn't arrive:
- Contact us within 7 days of expected delivery
- We'll investigate and resolve the issue
- Replacement or refund will be issued

## International Shipping

We currently ship within India only. For international orders, please contact us at thestatusring@gmail.com.
